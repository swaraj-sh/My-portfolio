UI/UX DESIGN

What is UI/UX Design?

UI(User Interface) is the look and feel of a product—the buttons, the colors, the typography. It's the visual part. UX(User Experience) is the overall feel of the product—how easy is it to use? Is it logical? Does it solve the user's problem?

Why Do You Need It?

A beautiful app that is confusing to use will fail. A simple app that is ugly will also fail. You need both. Good UI/UX design is what separates successful products from frustrating ones. It's the key to keeping users happy, engaged, and coming back.
